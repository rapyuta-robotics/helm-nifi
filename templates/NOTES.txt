kubectl port-forward -n {{ .Release.Namespace}} svc/{{ .Release.Name }} {{ $port }}:{{ $port }}

...and point your web browser to http{{ if .Values.properties.httpsPort }}s{{ end }}://localhost:{{ $port }}/nifi/
